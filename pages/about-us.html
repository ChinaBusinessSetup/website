How We Work

Structured steps from first review to operation

We use a staged process so clients understand the route before spending time preparing documents or arranging filings.

1. Case review

We review your business model, shareholder structure, target city, products or services and expected operating timeline.

2. Route planning

We compare entity type, business scope, registered address, capital plan, license needs and tax consequences.

3. Document checklist

We confirm what shareholders, directors and local contacts need to prepare before registration or changes.

4. China-side coordination

We coordinate registration, license, bank, tax, accounting or platform setup steps with practical progress updates.

5. Final review

We check key details such as company name, scope, address, tax category, document consistency and next obligations.

6. Ongoing support

We help with tax filings, accounting, annual reporting, changes, licenses and follow-up compliance as the company grows.
